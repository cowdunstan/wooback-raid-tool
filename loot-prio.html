Loot prio

Pick tonight’s signup and the raid, and the guild loot sheets turn into a per-boss prio list: for every item, the characters signed up that hold priority, in the sheet’s own order. Black Temple and Hyjal come from the P3 sheet, SSC and Tempest Keep from the P2 one. Pills flag anyone already wearing an item or who has won it before. Up top, “What you can roll on” pulls out where your own characters hold prio. Officers can mute a character on a single item with the ✕ beside their name.
